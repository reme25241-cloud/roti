About the Project

Roti E-Commerce Platform

Roti is an intelligent e-commerce solution tailored for local vendors and buyers looking to streamline the purchase of essential goods like food grains, vegetables, dairy, and bakery items. The platform ensures hyperlocal delivery, real-time inventory updates, and secure online payments.

  • Technology Stack: Android Studio, SQLite, XML, REST API
  • Key Features: Seller onboarding, dynamic pricing, inventory management, discount engine
  • Target Users: Local farmers, shopkeepers, individual buyers, delivery partners
  • Outcome: Empowered local sellers, improved food distribution chain, digital transformation of the grocery ecosystem
